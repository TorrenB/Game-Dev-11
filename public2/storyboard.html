Game Levels Information

Level 1

Hello human.
Level 1 is a 2D animation where you must avoid the terrifying deadly goldfish. Has 5 mini levels with each one getting harder as you move on. Upon the completion of level 5, you will be directed to the true level 2.

Level 2

Level 2 is a story based game that was built in Twine. This game is designed to be challenging and logic based. You will be needing a calculator and/or the help of the web to help you along your journey. Upon completing this, you will be directed to the best geography game known to mankind.

Level 3

Level 3 is where you will be put to the test on your flag knowledge. Know all the flags? Easy. Know none of them? Intense challenge. Very few manage to get this far. If you do somehow manage to succeed, be sure to show your accomplishment with those around as you have proven that you are superior.
