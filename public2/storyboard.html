Game Levels Information

Level 1

Welcome to Level 1!
In this stage, you will experience a fun 2D animation where your task is to skillfully avoid a dangerous goldfish. The level consists of five mini-stages, each becoming slightly more challenging. Upon completing the fifth stage, you will unlock Level 2.

Level 2

Level 2 is an intriguing narrative-driven game created using Twine. This level will test your problem-solving skills and logical thinking. You may find a calculator and online resources helpful during this engaging adventure. Once you complete this level, you will be directed to one of the best-known geography games available.

Level 3

In Level 3, your knowledge of national flags will be put to the test. If you are familiar with them, you may find this level straightforward. However, if you are less confident, it could pose a significant challenge. Few players reach this milestone, so if you succeed, be sure to celebrate your achievement!
