Game Levels Information

Level 1

Welcome, esteemed player.
Level 1 presents a captivating 2D animation experience where you must skillfully navigate and evade the formidable challenges posed by a deadly goldfish. This level comprises five mini-stages, each progressively increasing in difficulty. Successful completion of the fifth stage will grant you access to the authentic Level 2.

Level 2

Level 2 unfolds as an engaging, narrative-driven game crafted in Twine. This immersive experience is designed to challenge your cognitive abilities and logical reasoning. A calculator and possibly online resources will be essential allies on this intricate journey. Upon successfully completing this level, you will be guided towards one of the most acclaimed geography games known to humanity.

Level 3

Level 3 tests your knowledge of national flags, offering a challenge that varies significantly based on your existing familiarity. For those well-versed in global symbolism, this may be a straightforward task. Conversely, for those less familiar, it becomes a formidable challenge. Only a select few will reach this milestone; if you succeed, be sure to share your achievement, as you will have distinguished yourself among your peers.
